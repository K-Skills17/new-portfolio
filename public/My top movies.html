The Best Animes According To Stephen

My Top 5 Amines Of All Time

5. Attack On Titan

The story line is a little confusing but the animation is awesome and you have to pay extra attention to understant what is going on in the series

My Favorite Characters

- Eren Yeager
- Levi Ackerman

It is an anime i will watch again and again if i had the time to do so.

4. Jujutsu Kaisen

The character development and the powers in this series are top notch. The animation is in a world of its own.

However I think the writers made Gojo so famous that he outshines the main character

My Favorite Characters

- Saturo Gojo
- Ryomen Sukuna
- Yuji Itadori

I have already watched this anime more than one time and I would absolutely go back to watch again. Even if just the fight scenes which i consider amazing.
